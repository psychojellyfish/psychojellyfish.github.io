Geist Notes by Matt Buckley

Submitted by Lucas on Fri, 07/13/2012 - 22:55

GEIST NOTES

-Matt Buckley

FUNNY THINGS THAT DIDN’T MAKE A LOT OF SENSE

  • ·        fighting demons with fire extinguisher
  • ·        female character suits up for battle in surgery room
  • ·        possessed humans somehow can’t talk, need to communicate with hand signs.
  • ·        People’s reactions to scaring usually pretty funny: running away, splashing face with cold water, screaming and curling up.
  • ·        Terrible dialogue, Bryson (chapter four) "i don't know what's going on, but it must have something to do with this ghost business i keep hearing about"
  • ·        Unbelievably flat characters: nobody is ever really developed as a character or motivations delved into.
  • ·        Killing a “required host” ends the game, which was obviously done for linearity purposes, but doesn’t make sense in the context of the game world itself.

QUESTIONS/INCONSISTENCIES

  • ·        In the first chapter, one of your squadmates gets possessed and kills a bunch of your guys, but you never find out what happened to him.
  • ·        Raimi is a ghost, but he’s still held down by gravity.
  • ·        Ghost “dies” after being outside of host for extended period. Has to possess something to regain health.
  • ·        Ghosts eat chlorophyll? As a spirit, able to replenish health by absorbing plants.
  • ·        Volks Corporation: what the hell do they do? How do they get funding for machines that rip people’s spirits out?
  • ·        Bryson clearly gets helicopter shot down and then magically appears alive at the end, saying he’s not easy to kill.

OTHER COMPLAINTS

  • ·        For the scaring sequences, you always had one set method and order to scare someone, very linear and did not allow for creativity. Caused a lot of delays in my progress cause I needed to figure out what the F to do.
  • ·        Glitches and weird stuff: unresponsive controls, pipe tunnels that don’t lead anywhere.
  • ·        When GiGi dies in the flashback she falls about five feet out of a tree and then just dies,
  • ·        Really uncreative boss names: usually says “Boss” or “Creature.” The same tentacle monster demon is the same boss that is fought like three times.
  • ·        Never explains what the creatures from dark rift want/where final battle takes place.

PRAISES

  • ·        Fresh concept in terms of controlling a ghost and possession.

OTHER TALKING POINTS

  • ·        Overall thoughts on game: Some good ideas, some failed execution, overall worth checking out.
  • ·        Would you like to see a sequel?
  • ·        If you were a ghost, what would you do?
