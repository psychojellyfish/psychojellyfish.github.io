The Highwaymen

The Highwaymen: a post-apocalyptic adventure through the recovering wastes of Upstate New York. Three travelers find themselves the guests of a man bent on the destruction of their enemies: an enslaving cult relentless in tracking down its escapees. The approaching conflict will not only threaten the fragile bonds of trust between them, but the surviving remnants of post-apocalyptic civilization as a whole.

  

- Winner of Outstanding Theme Song in a Drama (Keith Kaufman) at the Los Angeles Web Series Festival 2014. 

- Official Selection at the Austin Web Festival 2014.

- Official Selection at the Miami Web Festival 2014.
